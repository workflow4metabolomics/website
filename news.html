regularly provides trainings for the community. In particular, we provide yearly bring-your-own data trainings, and regularly propose workshops at conferences.

Workflow4Experimenters

Each year, the W4M team organize a training school dedicated to the use of W4M infrastructure and analyze your own data (LC-MS, GC-MS and NMR data). This course (in English) is composed of an online theoretical part following by a one-week tutoring on-site session. During this practical part, you learn how to process your own data with the help of tutors, from W4M and close partners.

For more information, contact us at workflow4metabolomics@proton.me or visit the latest W4E event page below.
